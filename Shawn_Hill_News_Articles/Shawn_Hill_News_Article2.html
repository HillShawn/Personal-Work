Value investor Bill Nygren told CNBC on Wednesday that he saw opprotunities in the market even as the S$P 500 continues to set new record highs.

Nygren said on "Squawk on the Street" that there was "a lot of value in the market today" even as part of the market remind him of the tech bubble two decades ago.

"The P/E spread has gotten enormous between the highest priced stocks and the lowest price, reminiscent of where things were in '99," Nygren said.
